Overview

From January through August 2025, our call center team successfully conducted 88,425 calls reaching 25,365 unique contacts across 8 months of operations. With an impressive 63.6% reach rate, we engaged thousands of prospective learners across multiple programs. Among those reached, 12.8% expressed continued interest in enrollment, while 5.1% were actively considering their options. This comprehensive analysis reveals strong operational effectiveness with January achieving the highest reach rate of 72.0%, demonstrating the quality-focused approach that drives our success.

Key Insights

Notable findings from the 8-month analysis period

1

Exceptional Reach Rate

Out of a total of 88,425 calls made, 63.6% were successfully reached and spoken to. This demonstrates strong operational effectiveness and quality contact management.

2

Engagement Quality

Among those reached, 12.8% expressed continued interest, while 5.1% said they were still considering their options. This represents 10,028 engaged prospects ready for enrollment.

3

Delay Reasons Analysis

"Other" reasons account for 51.8% of enrollment delays, indicating diverse circumstances. Busy schedule (33.3%) and forgetting (13.4%) are the primary actionable reasons. Automated reminders and flexible scheduling could address these specific barriers.

4

January Excellence

January achieved the highest reach rate of 72.0% with 35.5% interest among reached contacts. This month serves as the gold standard for operational quality and effectiveness.

Monthly Summary

Key activities by month

January 2025 - Excellent Start
Best performing month with 72.0% reach rate. Made 9,656 calls reaching 6,950 contacts. 35.5% expressed interest - highest engagement of the year.
February 2025 - Strong Continuation
Maintained excellence with 69.8% reach rate. Conducted 7,957 calls reaching 5,552 contacts. Interest rate at 32.2% showing consistent quality.
March 2025 - Sustained Momentum
Achieved 66.6% reach rate with 9,123 calls and 6,073 contacts reached. Interest at 30.0% demonstrating ongoing engagement strength.
April 2025 - Volume Increase
10,605 calls with 65.5% reach rate (6,943 reached). Interest dropped to 15.7%, indicating potential need for list quality review.
May 2025 - Focused Operations
Smaller scale with 3,061 calls, 63.0% reach rate (1,928 reached). Maintained solid reach effectiveness despite reduced volume.
June 2025 - Recovery Phase
6,215 calls with 55.1% reach rate (3,427 reached). Slight dip in reach rate, focus on quality improvement needed.
July 2025 - High Volume Push
Peak volume month with 25,046 calls. Reached 15,397 contacts (61.5%). Volume surge while maintaining above-60% reach rate.
August 2025 - Strong Finish
16,762 calls with 59.6% reach rate (9,987 reached). Sustained high volume operations while maintaining solid performance.
