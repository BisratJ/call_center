## üí° KEY STRATEGIC INSIGHTS

### 1. REACH EFFECTIVENESS TRENDS

**Finding:** Reach rate inversely correlates with call volume

- **High reach months** (January: 51.7%, April: 30.4%) = Lower call volumes
- **Low reach months** (June: 9.5%, July: 10.7%) = Higher call volumes

**Implication:** Quality suffers when quantity increases beyond capacity

### 2. DATA QUALITY CONCERNS

**Critical Gaps Identified:**

- 63.6% of calls have "Unknown" status
- Interest level data missing for May-August
- Delay reasons only captured for 7,846 contacts

**Impact:** Limited ability to analyze effectiveness and optimize strategies

**Recommendation:** Implement mandatory data entry fields for all calls

### 3. ENGAGEMENT QUALITY

**Positive Indicators:**

- 11.3% of reached contacts show continued interest
- 5.1% are actively considering enrollment
- Combined positive engagement: 16.4% of reached contacts

**Concern:** 72.5% of reached contacts have no interest level recorded

### 4. OPERATIONAL BOTTLENECKS

**Busy Schedule (32.9% of delays):**

- Indicates timing/flexibility issues
- **Action:** Offer evening/weekend enrollment options

**Forgetting (14.5% of delays):**

- Process complexity or lack of reminders
- **Action:** Automated follow-up sequences

### 5. SEASONAL PATTERNS

**Q1 (Jan-Mar): High Quality Era**

- Best reach rates (18.5%-51.7%)
- Consistent interest tracking (22.5%-32.4%)
- Focused, manageable volumes

**Q2-Q3 (Apr-Aug): Volume Surge Era**

- Massive scale-up in calls
- Reach rates declined to 9.5%-14.5%
- Data quality degraded
- **Implication:** Infrastructure couldn't support volume

## üîç APPENDIX: METHODOLOGY

### Data Sources

- **Primary:** Agents Assigned sheets from 263 campaign Excel files
- **Period:** January 1, 2025 - August 31, 2025
- **Scope:** All agent tabs (Abeba, Nati, Heamen, Tsedenia, Yosef, Girum, Dagmawi, etc.)

### Metrics Definitions

**Unique Contacts:** Deduplicated count based on phone number or email  
**Total Calls:** All call attempts recorded in agent sheets  
**Reached:** Calls where contact answered and conversation occurred  
**Reach Rate:** (Reached / Total Calls) √ó 100  
**Interested %:** (Interested / Reached) √ó 100

### Call Outcome Categories

- **Reached:** Contact answered, conversation completed
- **Not Picking Up:** Phone rang, no answer
- **Busy:** Line busy during attempt
- **Phone Not Working:** Invalid/switched off/non-functioning number
- **Unknown:** No status recorded
- **Other:** Alternative outcomes

### Limitations

1. **Data Quality:** 63.6% of calls lack status information
2. **Interest Tracking:** Discontinued after April 2025
3. **Reason Categorization:** 51.2% marked as "Other"
4. **Consistency:** Varying data entry standards across months
